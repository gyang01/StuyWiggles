Welcome to the Emotion Stock Market!

Constructed by Georgiana Yang, Mengdi Lin, Victoria Stempel and Zuzanna Rybicka



Purpose:

The Emotion Stock Market is a traditional stock market game with a little twist -- instead of investing in the performance of companies, users invest in the prevalence of emotions! The value of a given emotion is calculated based on its usage in Twitter in comparison to other emotions. These values are charted and graphed on a daily basis in the Market. Users are able to buy emotions with money. A user can borrow money from the Central Bank. The Central Bank trades money for a user's soul (each user starts out with 100% of soul intact). Users also can buy back soul.

How To Play

  1. Make an account & log in (if you haven't already)!
  2. Go to Stock Market to start trading! Stock prices will vary daily.
  3. Bankrupt? Sell your soul for $$$! >:D
  4. Save yourself! Buy your soul back! O:)
  5. Remember to monitor your stocks, money and soul! (Check out your Profile)!
